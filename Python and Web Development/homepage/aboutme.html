Intro

My name is Mubeen Padaniya and I am currently studying Bachelors in Space Engineering at Lassonde School of Engineering (as seen in the picture) at York University. I am currently in my third year and as many of my colleague, I hope to contribute to the international aerospace industry.

However I am also deeply interested in the fields of robotics, automation, data science (particularly big data) and geomatics. Since space engineering is a systems engineering field, I get a taste of all the other majors.

Hobbies

My biggest hobbies are cycling, reading and cooking. I started reading at the early age of 5 and soon become a regular at my local library. I attribute my knowledge, thinking and awareness to this. My favourite non-fiction book is the Grand Design by Stephen Hawking and for fiction, my love goes to the Lord of the Rings series.

I love spend the warmer times of the year outdoors, speeding down the trails on my mountain bike. Its a fast, sustainable and healthy way to travel that is also a great deal of fun.

Lastly, out of necessity I learned to cook. I never thought it as a chore and often would find new recipes to try. It has also taught me paitence, attention to detail and acceptance of failure. My favourite dishes are lasagna, kadai paneer and burritos.

My Goals

I am very imaginative individual and I always try to aim for the higher echelon. One of my most important goals is to eventually work with a space based company, primarily on launch vehicles. I have been intrigued by various propolusion designed and have been really interested in beating Earth's deep gravity well in a sustainable and fast manner. My current studies a part of the process that will get me there.

Another one of my goals is to found a startup. I am always brimming up with novel ideas that i believe will better people's lives. Entreprenuership gives me a chance to have a wider impact on the world and take charge of my own creations. My unique experience studying entrepreneurship in Israel broadened my horizons, emboldened me and bestowed me the key skills and knowledge imperative to make a successful business.
